About Me

My name is Ernesto Martinez, and I am currently a student at Yearup for the Software development track. Where I am learning HTML, CSS, Javascript and Python.

I was raised and born in Mexico, specifically in Asuncion Ocotlan Oaxaca, a small town in Southeast Mexico where most of my village's people speak Zapotec, a native language. I moved to the United States in 2012 when I was 17. I finished high school in Monroe, WA, and eventually continued my education at Edmonds Community College in 2014. I studied music; I dropped off two years later, in 2016, to enter the workforce.
